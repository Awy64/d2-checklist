Destiny 2 Checklist

Donate /r/destinychecklistnet Source Code
I aim to serve the same needs that helped folks with Destiny 1 at DestinyChecklist.net. Main goals are:
  • Checking on you and your friend's progress, rewards and completions
  • Supporting clan tracking and rewards
  • Helping you progress in PvE

I've been lazy putting this page together, if you want more info you can also see my about page on DestinyChecklist.net (my D1 hobby site) for most of the important info. This site is open sourced on Bitbucket, and actively supported via Reddit and Twitter.

PS: If you don't like ads (or want a dark theme) just pop over to the settings page and disable them. The site will remember your settings in the future.
