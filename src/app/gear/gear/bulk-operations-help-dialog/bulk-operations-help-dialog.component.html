Bulk Operation Help

Sync Locks willl update the lock state of your items to match their tags. Upgrade/Keep items will be locked. Infuse/Junk items will be unlocked.

Clear Inv clears all non-equipped items marked Infuse/Junk from your most recent character.

Shard mode clears all non-equipped items from your most recent character, then transfers all the junk that will fit to them, so you can quickly and safely shard things in bulk.

Cheap Upgrade mode clears all non-equipped items from your most recent character, then transfers as many sets of items where you have Infuse and Upgrade of the same type (for cheap infusion).

Weapon Grind mode clears all non-equipped weapons from your most recent character, then transfers in all Deepsight weapons and Crafted weapons < lvl 20
