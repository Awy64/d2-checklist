Wildcard Filter Info

The wildcard filter will filter by item name, along with masterwork, mod and perk names. It also has a few special commands (some of which match DIM's filters)

  • AND let's you type in multiple filters at once. For example godroll AND rampage or even alloy AND rampage AND zen
  • OR let's you type in multiple filters at once. For example solar OR void (OR causes AND to be ignored, they cannot be combined)
  • ! adds a NOT filter to your query. !pulse monitor will show guns missing Pulse Monitor. !zen AND rampage
    • will show guns with rampage but not zen moment. This also works with "magic" words below, so you can use !targetperk to find all your armor missing features you care about (perhaps to mark it all as junk)
  • is:wishlist or godroll Show only items that match a god roll from a curated list of rolls (see "Utilities" button for more info)
  • is:wishlistpve is:wishlistpvp or godrollpvp godrollpve Selected by PvE vs PvP god rolls
  • fixme Show items with godroll perks that aren't currently active
  • is:masterwork Has a fully upgraded masterwork socket
  • is:hasmod Has a mod applied (so be careful infusing this into something else, since you'll lose the mod
  • random Is a random roll, and thus cannot be restored from Collections
  • fixed Is a fixed roll, you can always grab it from collections
  • targetperk Armor with a perk that you've targetted
  • freshtarget Targetted perk that isn't already equipped (unless the item itself is equipped)
  • manytargetperks Highlights gear that has more than one target perk
  • onetargetperk Highlights gear that has ONLY one target perk
